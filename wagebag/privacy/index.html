Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how WageBag ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information Collection and Use

WageBag does not collect or store any personal data from users. All information such as your spending, shopping lists, and wage details is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

WageBag does not use any third-party services that collect user data. Any AI analysis or budget suggestions are processed using Google's Gemini API, but no personal identifiers are sent—only anonymized item and wage data.

Data Security

We take your privacy seriously. All your data is stored securely on your device. You are in control of your information, and it is never shared without your consent.

Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and you are encouraged to review it periodically.
